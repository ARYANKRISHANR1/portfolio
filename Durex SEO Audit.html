Durex India: A Strategic SEO & Technical Performance Audit

Overview

This project involved a comprehensive SEO audit of durexindia.com to identify technical bottlenecks and content gaps in the highly competitive sexual wellness market. Despite having high organic traffic, the objective was to solve for suboptimal load speeds and "failed" Core Web Vitals that were likely impacting user experience and long-term search rankings. The audit focuses on moving the platform from a purely transactional site to an educational authority in the sexual wellness space.

My Role & What I Did

  • Technical Performance Auditing: Used Core Web Vitals to diagnose critical speed issues, identifying that the mobile site was taking over 4.6s for its Largest Contentful Paint (LCP), far exceeding the target threshold of 3s.
  • Competitor Benchmarking: Conducted a comparative analysis against rivals like Manforce and Skore, evaluating Authority Scores (AS) and keyword gaps to map Durex's market positioning.
  • Keyword & Intent Analysis: Identified a "Conversion Paradox" where the site ranked well for brand terms (e.g., "Durex India") but missed significant traffic opportunities for high-volume informational keywords related to sexual wellness.
  • Content Gap Analysis: Discovered that while Durex has a strong social media presence, the website failed to effectively leverage this content to build trust and educational value beyond product pages.

The Outcome

I delivered a 5-point strategic roadmap to stabilize and grow Durex’s digital footprint:

  • Technical SEO: High-priority optimization of structured data and page load speeds to pass Core Web Vitals assessments.
  • Content Expansion: Proposed a new educational content vertical to capture "Top of the Funnel" informational search intent.
  • Mobile-First UX: Targeted a 60%+ improvement in mobile traffic retention by addressing failed UX metrics.
  • Social Integration: Developed a medium-priority plan to sync high-performing social media storytelling directly onto the D2C platform.
