Overview

Not Average is a lifestyle and apparel movement born out of the belief that mediocrity is a choice. The market is flooded with "average" fast fashion that lacks soul; Not Average was created to fill that gap. It is a brand for the high-performers, the ultramarathoners, and the dreamers who refuse to settle for the status quo. We don’t just sell clothes; we provide the uniform for those committed to surpassing their current limits.

My Role & Execution

  • Founder & Creative Director: Conceptualized the brand identity, naming, and the "surpassing" philosophy that drives every design.
  • Product Development & Sourcing: Spearheaded R&D for high-quality fabrics. Developed a custom Arduino-based color-sensing tool to ensure apparel perfectly matches iconic sneakers (like Jordan colorways), solving a common frustration.
  • Consumer Psychology Integration: Applied psychological principles of "enclothed cognition" to design apparel that actively boosts the wearer's confidence and focus.
  • Digital Strategy & Content: Managed end-to-end digital presence, producing high-energy video content and building a highly disciplined community on Instagram.

The Outcome

Not Average has evolved from a classroom concept into a living brand with a dedicated following. We successfully bridged the gap between premium streetwear and motivational gear. By maintaining a 100% focus on the "Not Average" lifestyle, the brand sees consistent engagement from a community of creators and athletes who value precision, quality, and the relentless pursuit of being better than they were yesterday.
