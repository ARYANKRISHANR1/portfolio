Tata.ev: Re-Imagining Brand Identity for a Post-Demerger Era

Overview

Following the strategic demerger of Tata’s passenger vehicle business, this project proposes a ground-up rebranding of its Electric Vehicle (EV) division. Moving away from "legacy branding" that heavily borrows from the parent Tata brand, Tata.ev is positioned as a standalone, tech-forward leader in sustainable mobility. The project addresses the "Conversion Paradox" in brand identity—shifting from a purely functional fuel-type identifier (".ev" suffix) to an emotive, lifestyle-driven brand experience.

My Role & What I Did

  • Comprehensive Brand Audit: Conducted a deep dive into existing brand elements (Logo, Tagline, Color Palette) to identify "legacy dependencies" that limit global competitiveness.
  • Brand Resonance Analysis: Applied the Customer-Based Brand Equity (CBBE) model to identify a weakness in "Resonance" and "Feelings," recommending a shift from a focus on security to one of excitement and community.
  • Strategic Positioning: Defined new Points of Difference (PODs) centered on cutting-edge technology and a vibrant user community, while maintaining Tata’s traditional Points of Parity (POPs) like safety and reliability.

Visual & Verbal Identity Redesign:

  • Logo: Designed a bold, modern "T" that incorporates negative space to symbolize a path moving forward.
  • Color Palette: Proposed a high-contrast shift from Teal Blue to a Black and Electric Green scheme to represent the intersection of sophisticated technology and sustainable energy.
  • Tagline: Replaced the functional "Go.ev" with the more emotive and visionary "Powering Tomorrow".

The Outcome

The project delivers a 360-degree "Branded House" architecture for the new entity. The resulting prototype—Tata.ev—successfully transitions the brand from a trusted Indian manufacturer to a dynamic, global tech-forward movement. The redesign provides a blueprint for unlocking the brand's future potential and building a deeply resonant connection with the next generation of eco-conscious, tech-savvy drivers.
