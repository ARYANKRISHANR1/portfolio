Automated 3D Warehouse

Overview

Developed as a collaborative project for a Supply Chain Management course, this initiative aimed to visualize the future of logistics through a fully automated warehouse ecosystem. The project addresses the need for maximum efficiency and "lights-out" operations in modern e-commerce.

My Role & What I Did

  • 3D Environmental Design: Mastered Blender within 24 hours to design and render a high-fidelity 3D model.
  • Systems Architecture: Designed the automated workflow, including autonomous truck docking and robotic integration.
  • Last-Mile Integration: Integrated a drone delivery system for seamless transition to final delivery.

The Outcome

The prototype effectively demonstrated how automation can handle the entire intake-to-delivery cycle, proving the feasibility of high-efficiency, autonomous logistics hubs.
